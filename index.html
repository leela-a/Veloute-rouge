Before You Order

Velouté Rouge is a pre-order–only bakery based in Oshawa, Ontario. All orders are available for pickup only — the exact pickup location will be provided in your confirmation email after checkout.

Please place your order at least 4 days in advance to allow time for ingredient preparation and baking. When weekly capacity is reached, new orders will temporarily close until the following week.

Pickup Hours:
Saturdays — 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM
Sundays — 1:00 PM to 9:00 PM
